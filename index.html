The Best Sports at BYU

Football

BYU's football program has had its ups and downs but ended the season of 2023 with some great effort and heart, despite close loses to Oklahoma and Oklahoma State. It's looking to have a great season this year with lots of options at quarterback and a better coaching staff.

Men's and Women's Rugby

Two very strong programs on both sides of the ball is men's and women's rugby, with womens rugby winning the national championship several years in a row and men's almost winning a national title in 2023.

Men's and Women's Volleyball

Volleyball at BYU on both the men's and women's teams is becoming increasingly better as they recieve top ten rankings each year, getting closer and closer to winning a national championship.

Men's Basketball

Since joining the Big 12 BYU basketball has shown that they belong as a competative team in the future. The possibility of contending for a Big 12 chamionship is also in the runnings as BYU basketball just hired coach Kevin Young, a previous NBA assistant coach.
